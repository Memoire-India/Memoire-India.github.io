About Us

We are Memoire, an Ahmedabad-based wedding photography and videography agency run by a team of creative professionals. With over 15 years of experience, we are dedicated hobbyists driven by creative pursuit and a passion for capturing beautiful moments and visual storytelling.

We cater to a niche clientele in Ahmedabad, Vadodara, Anand, Nadiad, Surat, Rajkot, and surrounding areas, undertaking a select number of weddings each winter season. This includes destination weddings in scenic locations like Udaipur and Goa, as well as projects across India and internationally. You can find us on Instagram and YouTube for a glimpse into our work and to get in touch!
